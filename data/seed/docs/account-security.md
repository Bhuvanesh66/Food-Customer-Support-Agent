# Account & Security

## Resetting your password

1. On the sign-in screen, tap **Forgot password?**.
2. Enter your account email. We send a reset link valid for **1 hour**.
3. Open the link and choose a new password (**minimum 8 characters, at least one number**).

If the email doesn't arrive within a few minutes, check spam/junk and confirm you used the email
tied to your account. If you signed up with your phone number, use **Sign in with code** instead —
we'll text you a one-time code.

## Signing in — email, phone, or social

You can sign in with your email + password, a one-time **SMS code** (if you signed up by phone), or
a linked **Google/Apple** account. Linked social logins can be managed under **Account → Security →
Connected accounts**.

## Updating your phone number or email

Change contact details under **Account → Profile**. You'll verify the new phone or email with a code
before it's saved. Your order history, credits, and saved addresses stay with the account.

## Managing delivery addresses

Add, edit, remove, or reorder saved addresses under **Account → Addresses**. For each address you
can save **gate codes, apartment/unit numbers, building names, and drop-off instructions** ("leave
at door", "call on arrival") so couriers find you every time. Set a default address used at checkout.

## Two-factor authentication (2FA)

Enable 2FA under **Account → Security → Two-Factor Authentication** for extra protection. We may
also require a one-time code for high-value orders, new devices, or unusual activity. If you lose
access to your 2FA method, use a backup code or contact support to verify your identity.

## Managing devices and sessions

Review where you're signed in under **Account → Security → Devices**. You can sign out an individual
device or all devices at once. Signing out everywhere forces re-authentication — useful if you lose
a phone.

## Unrecognized charges or account access

If you see an order you didn't place or suspect someone accessed your account:
1. **Change your password** immediately.
2. **Sign out unknown devices** under Account → Security → Devices.
3. **Contact support** — we can freeze the account, investigate unauthorized orders, and refund
   fraudulent charges after review.
Never share your password or one-time codes; FoodAssist AI staff will never ask for them.

## Privacy and your data

You control marketing preferences under **Account → Notifications** and can request a copy of your
data under **Account → Privacy**. We encrypt data in transit and at rest.

## Deleting your account

Request deletion under **Account → Privacy → Delete account**. Deletion is **permanent after a
30-day grace period** and removes your order history and saved data. Any outstanding account credit
or gift-card balance is **forfeited** on deletion, so use it first. Active subscriptions should be
cancelled before deleting.

## Notifications

Control order updates, courier messages, and promotions under **Account → Notifications**, per
channel (push, email, SMS). You can mute **promotional** notifications while keeping **order and
delivery** alerts on. "Do Not Disturb" hours silence non-critical push notifications.
