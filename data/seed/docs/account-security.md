# Account & Security

## Resetting your password

1. On the sign-in screen, tap **Forgot password?**.
2. Enter your account email. We'll send a reset link (valid for 1 hour).
3. Open the link and choose a new password (minimum 8 characters, one number).

If the email doesn't arrive within a few minutes, check spam and confirm you used the email tied to your account. If you signed up with your phone number, use **Sign in with code** instead.

## Updating your phone number or email

Change your contact details under **Account → Profile**. You'll verify the new phone or email with a code before it's saved. Your order history and credits stay with your account.

## Managing delivery addresses

Add, edit, or remove saved addresses under **Account → Addresses**. Add gate codes, apartment/unit numbers, and drop-off instructions so couriers can find you. Your default address is used at checkout unless you change it.

## Two-factor authentication

Enable 2FA under **Account → Security → Two-Factor Authentication** for an extra layer of protection. FoodAssist AI can also require a one-time code for high-value orders or new devices.

## Unrecognized charges or account access

If you see an order you didn't place or suspect someone accessed your account: change your password immediately, review **Account → Security → Devices** and sign out unknown devices, and contact support. We can freeze the account and investigate unauthorized orders.

## Deleting your account

You can request account deletion under **Account → Privacy → Delete account**. Deletion is permanent after a 30-day grace period and removes your order history and saved data. Any outstanding FoodAssist AI credit is forfeited on deletion.

## Notifications

Control order updates, promotions, and courier messages under **Account → Notifications**. You can mute promotional notifications while keeping order and delivery alerts on.
