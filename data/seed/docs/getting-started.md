# Getting Started with FoodAssist AI

FoodAssist AI is a food delivery app that connects you with local restaurants and grocery stores,
delivered to your door by couriers. This guide covers signing up, placing your first order, and the
basics of how the service works.

## Creating your account

1. Download **FoodAssist AI** from the App Store or Google Play, or go to **foodassist.app**.
2. Sign up with your **phone number or email**. We send a 6-digit verification code.
3. Add a **delivery address** — you can save several (Home, Work, etc.) with notes and gate codes.
4. Add a **payment method** to start ordering.

## Placing your first order

1. Confirm your delivery address so we show restaurants that deliver to you.
2. Browse by cuisine, search a dish, or pick from **Recommended for you**.
3. Add items to your cart, customize them, then tap **Checkout**.
4. Choose **ASAP** or **Schedule**, add delivery notes, apply any promo code, and confirm payment.
5. Track your order live on the map until it arrives.

## How delivery works

Once you order, the restaurant confirms and starts preparing. A nearby courier is assigned, picks up
your food, and brings it to you. You'll see each stage (Confirmed → Preparing → Picked up → On the
way → Delivered) and can message the courier after pickup. Delivery times are estimates that vary
with prep time, distance, traffic, and weather.

## Delivery areas and hours

FoodAssist AI operates in most major cities. Each restaurant shows its open hours, delivery radius,
and estimated delivery time. Some offer **pickup** if you'd rather collect the order yourself.
Availability depends on your delivery address.

## Pickup vs delivery

Choose **Pickup** on supported restaurants to skip delivery fees and collect your order yourself —
you'll get a ready-time estimate and can confirm at the counter with your name or order number.

## FoodAssist Plus (membership)

**FoodAssist Plus** is our membership at **$9.99/month**:
- **Free delivery** on orders over $15
- **Lower service fees**
- **Member-only offers**
The **first month is free** for new members. Cancel anytime under **Account → FoodAssist Plus**;
benefits last until the end of the billing period.

## Getting help

For anything the app doesn't resolve, open **Orders → Get help** on a specific order, or chat with
**Synapse AI**, our support assistant. It can answer questions about orders, delivery, payments,
refunds, your account, and restaurants — and connect you to a human agent when needed.

## Supported devices

FoodAssist AI works on iOS and Android apps and any modern web browser at foodassist.app. Your
account, orders, and credits sync across all of them.
