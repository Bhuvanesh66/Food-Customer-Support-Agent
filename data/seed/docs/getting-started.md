# Getting Started with FoodAssist AI

FoodAssist AI is a food delivery app that connects you with local restaurants and grocery stores, delivered to your door by couriers.

## Creating your account

1. Download the **FoodAssist AI** app from the App Store or Google Play, or go to **foodassist.app**.
2. Sign up with your phone number or email. We'll send a 6-digit verification code.
3. Add your delivery address — you can save multiple addresses (Home, Work, etc.).
4. Add a payment method to start ordering.

## Placing your first order

1. Enter or confirm your delivery address so we can show restaurants that deliver to you.
2. Browse by cuisine, search for a dish, or pick from **Recommended for you**.
3. Add items to your cart, then tap **Checkout**.
4. Choose a delivery time (ASAP or scheduled), add delivery notes, and confirm payment.
5. Track your order live on the map until it arrives.

## Delivery areas and hours

FoodAssist AI is available in most major cities. Restaurant hours vary; each restaurant shows its open hours and estimated delivery time. Some restaurants offer **pickup** if you'd rather collect your order yourself.

## FoodAssist Plus (membership)

FoodAssist Plus is our membership: **$9.99/month** for **free delivery** on orders over $15, lower service fees, and exclusive offers. You can cancel anytime from **Account → FoodAssist Plus**. The first month is free for new members.
