# Order Help — Tracking, Refunds & Resolutions (by order)

This guide explains what to do for a **specific order**. Customers can find their **order ID**
(format **FA-XXXX**) on the order screen or in their confirmation email. The support agent looks up
the live order with that ID and then applies the right procedure below.

## How to track a specific order

Ask for the **order ID** (e.g. FA-1001). With it, the agent can see the live status — Confirmed →
Preparing → Picked up → On the way → Delivered — plus the courier, ETA, and delivery address.
Tell the customer the **current status, the ETA, and the courier's name**. After pickup, the
customer can message the courier in-app.

## Late delivery (status: on the way, past ETA)

1. Look up the order and check the **current ETA vs the original estimate**.
2. If it's running late, reassure the customer and share the live status + courier.
3. If the order is **more than 15 minutes past the original estimate**, it qualifies for a **delay
   credit** — apply a small credit as goodwill and confirm the courier is en route.

## Missing item (status: delivered, item missing)

1. Confirm which item is missing from the delivered order.
2. The customer is eligible for an **item refund** for the missing item.
3. Offer **instant FoodAssist credit** or a **card refund (3–5 business days)** for that item's price.

## Wrong item (status: delivered, wrong item)

1. Confirm what was received vs ordered.
2. Eligible for a **full refund of the wrong item** or a **re-delivery** of the correct item.

## Food quality issue (cold, wilted, spilled, damaged)

1. Ask for details (a photo helps — the agent can analyze it).
2. Offer a **partial or full refund** depending on severity, plus a **goodwill credit**, and flag
   the restaurant.

## Order never arrived (status: delivered, not received)

1. Have the customer check around the door and with neighbors, and confirm the saved address.
2. The agent reviews the **courier's GPS drop-off**. If it doesn't match the address, refund in
   full or arrange a re-delivery.

## Payment failed (status: payment failed)

1. The order is **held, not cancelled**, and **no charge has settled**.
2. Ask the customer to **update their card** under Account → Payment and **retry**.
3. The most common cause is an **expired card**.

## Cancelled order

- **Free cancellation** applies only **before the restaurant starts preparing** — a full refund is
  issued automatically.
- Once preparing/cooking has started, free cancellation is no longer available.

## Refund procedure for an order (summary)

1. Look up the order and confirm the issue and **refund eligibility**.
2. Choose the refund method with the customer: **NomNom/FoodAssist credit (instant, never expires)**
   or **original payment method (3–5 business days)**.
3. Refunds: **full** for undelivered/wrong orders; **per-item** for missing items; **partial** for
   quality issues. Eligibility is reviewed per order under **Get help → Report an issue**.

## Example order IDs (demo)

- **FA-1001** — late delivery (on the way, ~12 min late)
- **FA-1002** — payment failed (expired card)
- **FA-1003** — delivered, missing item (garlic bread)
- **FA-1004** — preparing (cancellation no longer free)
- **FA-1005** — cancelled before prep (full refund)
- **FA-1006** — marked delivered but never received
- **FA-1007** — wrong item delivered
- **FA-1008** — quality issue (wilted/leaking)
