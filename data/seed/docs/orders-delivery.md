# Orders & Delivery

## Tracking your order

After you place an order, tap it under **Orders** to see live status: **Confirmed → Preparing → Picked up → On the way → Delivered**. You'll see your courier's location on the map and the estimated arrival time.

## My order is late

Delivery times are estimates and can change with restaurant prep times, traffic, and weather. If your order is significantly past the estimate:
- Check the live map — the courier may be on the way.
- You can message the courier in-app once the order is picked up.
- If it's more than 15 minutes late with no movement, contact support and we'll investigate or offer a credit.

## Missing or wrong items

If an item is missing or incorrect, go to **Orders → [your order] → Get help → Report an issue**, select the affected items, and choose a resolution. Approved issues are refunded to your original payment method or as FoodAssist AI credit, usually within minutes for credit or 3–5 business days for card refunds.

## Order never arrived

If your order is marked delivered but you didn't receive it, first check around your door and with neighbors, and confirm your delivery address was correct. Then report it under **Get help → Order never arrived**. We'll review the courier's GPS drop-off and make it right.

## Cancelling an order

You can cancel for free while the order is still **Confirmed** (before the restaurant starts preparing it) from **Orders → Cancel order**. Once the restaurant has started cooking, the order can't be cancelled for free because the food is already being made. If you need to cancel after that, contact support — partial credit may apply at our discretion.

## Changing your delivery address after ordering

The address can only be changed before the courier picks up the order, and only to a nearby location. Tap **Orders → Edit delivery details**. Large address changes aren't possible once a courier is assigned.

## Contacting your courier

Once your order is picked up, a **Message courier** button appears on the order screen. Couriers can see your delivery notes (gate codes, apartment number, "leave at door").

## Tipping

You can add a tip at checkout or after delivery from the order screen. 100% of tips go to your courier. You can adjust the tip up to 1 hour after delivery.
