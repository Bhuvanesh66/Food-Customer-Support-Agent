# Orders & Delivery

## Tracking your order

After you place an order, open the **Orders** tab and tap your active order to see live status:
**Confirmed → Preparing → Picked up → On the way → Delivered**. You'll see your courier's
location on a live map and an estimated arrival time that updates as conditions change. You also
get push notifications at each stage (configurable under Account → Notifications).

## My order is late

Delivery estimates account for restaurant prep time, courier travel, traffic, and weather, so the
ETA can move. If your order is running late:
- Check the live map — the courier may already be on the way with an updated ETA.
- Once the order is **Picked up**, a **Message courier** button appears so you can contact them.
- If the order is **more than 15 minutes past the latest estimate** with no movement, contact
  support from **Orders → Get help** and we'll investigate, nudge the courier, or offer a credit.
- During peak times (Friday/Saturday evenings, holidays) estimates are naturally longer.

## Missing or wrong items

If an item is missing, incorrect, or you received someone else's order:
1. Go to **Orders → [your order] → Get help → Report an issue**.
2. Select the affected items and choose a resolution.
3. Adding a photo speeds up approval.
Approved issues are refunded as **FoodAssist AI credit (applied instantly)** or to your
**original payment method (3–5 business days)**. For a completely wrong order, we refund in full
and help you re-order.

## Food arrived cold, damaged, or spilled

Report it under **Get help → Report an issue → Food quality**. Photos help. Depending on severity
we issue a partial or full refund and may add a goodwill credit. Quality complaints are also shared
with the restaurant to prevent repeats.

## Order never arrived (marked delivered)

If the app says **Delivered** but you didn't receive it:
1. Check around your door, lobby, mailroom, and with neighbors.
2. Confirm the saved delivery address and unit number are correct.
3. Report under **Get help → Order never arrived**.
We review the courier's GPS drop-off location and photo (if taken) and make it right — usually a
full refund or re-delivery.

## Cancelling an order

- **Free cancellation** is available while the order is still **Confirmed** — before the restaurant
  starts preparing it — via **Orders → Cancel order**.
- **Once the restaurant starts cooking**, the order can't be cancelled for free because the food is
  already being made. You can still contact support; partial credit may apply at our discretion.
- **Scheduled orders** can be cancelled free any time before the restaurant begins preparing.
- If the restaurant cancels (too busy, closed, out of an item), you're refunded automatically and
  notified.

## Changing your delivery address after ordering

You can edit the delivery address only **before the courier picks up** the order, and only to a
**nearby location** (a small adjustment, not a different part of town). Go to **Orders → Edit
delivery details**. Once a courier is assigned and en route, large changes aren't possible —
contact the courier via **Message courier** for minor drop-off instructions.

## Contacting your courier

After pickup, a **Message courier** button appears on the order screen. Couriers can see your saved
delivery notes — gate codes, apartment/unit number, "leave at door", "call on arrival". Add these
under Account → Addresses so every order includes them.

## Tipping

You can add a tip at checkout, or **adjust it up to 1 hour after delivery** from the order screen.
**100% of tips go to your courier.** Tipping is optional but appreciated, especially in bad weather
or for large/heavy orders.

## Group orders

Start a **Group order** from a restaurant page and share the link so others add items to the same
cart. The organizer reviews and pays for the whole order, or splits the bill if the restaurant
supports bill-splitting. There's a per-restaurant item limit on very large group orders.

## Scheduling an order for later

At checkout, choose **Schedule** instead of ASAP and pick a delivery window (e.g. 7:00–7:15 PM).
Great for ordering before a restaurant opens or planning ahead. You can edit or cancel a scheduled
order any time before the restaurant starts preparing it.

## Order limits and large orders

Very large orders may require a longer prep window and occasionally a second courier. Some
restaurants set a maximum order size; if so, it's shown at checkout. Catering-style orders are
best scheduled in advance.

## Rating your order

After delivery, rate the **food** and the **courier** separately under **Orders → Rate**. Low
ratings with a reason help the restaurant and us improve and may trigger a support follow-up if
something went wrong.
