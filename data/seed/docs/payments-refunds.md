# Payments & Refunds

## Payment methods

FoodAssist AI accepts credit and debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and FoodAssist AI gift cards. Add or remove methods under **Account → Payment**. You can set a default payment method for faster checkout.

## Why was my payment declined?

The most common reasons are an **expired card**, insufficient funds, or your bank flagging the transaction. Update or re-add your card under **Account → Payment → Add card**, then retry. If it still fails, contact your bank to authorize FoodAssist AI.

## Understanding the charges

Your total includes: item prices, a **delivery fee** (waived for FoodAssist Plus on orders over $15), a **service fee**, any applicable taxes, and your tip. A temporary **authorization hold** may appear before the final charge settles — this is normal and clears within a few days.

## Refunds

If you're eligible for a refund (missing items, order issues, eligible cancellations), you choose **FoodAssist AI credit** (applied instantly) or a **refund to your original payment method** (3–5 business days). Refund eligibility is reviewed per order under **Get help → Report an issue**.

## Promo codes and credits

Enter promo codes at checkout under **Add promo code**. Credits apply automatically to your next eligible order. Promo codes can't be combined unless stated, and most have a minimum order value and expiry date. FoodAssist AI credit never expires.

## Double charged

If you see two charges for one order, one is likely a temporary authorization hold that will drop off automatically. If both charges settle, report it under **Get help → Billing** and we'll refund the duplicate.

## FoodAssist Plus billing

FoodAssist Plus renews monthly at **$9.99** on your signup date. Manage or cancel under **Account → FoodAssist Plus → Manage membership**. Cancelling stops future renewals; you keep benefits until the end of the current billing period. The membership fee itself is non-refundable once a billing period starts.
