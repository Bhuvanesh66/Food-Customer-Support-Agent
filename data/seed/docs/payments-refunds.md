# Payments & Refunds

## Payment methods

FoodAssist AI accepts:
- **Credit and debit cards** (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover, RuPay)
- **Apple Pay** and **Google Pay**
- **FoodAssist AI gift cards** and **account credit**
- **UPI** and popular wallets in supported regions

Add, remove, or set a default method under **Account → Payment**. A default method speeds up
checkout. You can split a payment between account credit and a card (credit is applied first).

## Why was my payment declined?

The most common reasons:
- **Expired card** — update it under **Account → Payment → Add card**, then retry.
- **Insufficient funds** or a daily limit on the card.
- **Bank fraud check** flagged the transaction — approve it in your banking app or call your bank.
- **Billing details mismatch** (wrong ZIP/postal or CVV).
After fixing, retry checkout. If it still fails, try another payment method or contact support.

## Understanding the charges

Your total is made up of:
- **Item prices** (set by the restaurant)
- **Delivery fee** — varies by distance and demand; **waived for FoodAssist Plus** on orders over $15
- **Service fee** — a small percentage that helps operate the platform
- **Taxes** — as required by local law
- **Tip** — optional, 100% to the courier
A small **authorization hold** may appear before the final charge settles — this is your bank
verifying the card, not a real charge, and it clears within a few days.

## Why is there a pending charge that disappeared?

That's a temporary **authorization hold**. Banks place it to confirm the card is valid. It is **not**
a second charge and drops off automatically within 1–5 business days. The real charge settles when
your order completes.

## Double charged / charged twice

If you see two charges for one order, one is almost always the pending authorization hold that will
disappear on its own. If **both charges actually settle**, report it under **Get help → Billing** and
we'll refund the duplicate (3–5 business days to card, or instant as credit).

## Refunds — how they work

If you're eligible (missing items, quality issues, eligible cancellations, undelivered order), you
choose:
- **FoodAssist AI credit** — applied **instantly**, never expires, used automatically on your next order.
- **Original payment method** — takes **3–5 business days** to appear (sometimes up to 10 for some banks).
Refund eligibility is reviewed per order under **Get help → Report an issue**. Full refunds apply
for undelivered or completely wrong orders; partial refunds for missing items or quality issues.

## Refund didn't arrive

Card refunds take 3–5 business days (occasionally longer depending on your bank). If it's been
longer: check the **same card/account** you paid with, look for a credit from "FoodAssist", and
confirm the refund was approved under **Orders → [order] → status**. Still missing? Contact support
with the order ID and we'll trace it.

## Promo codes and credits

Enter promo codes at checkout via **Add promo code**. Notes:
- Most codes have a **minimum order value** and an **expiry date**.
- Codes generally **can't be combined** unless explicitly stated.
- New-customer codes work only on a first order.
- **Account credit** applies automatically to your next eligible order and **never expires**.
If a code won't apply, check the minimum, expiry, and whether it's restricted to certain
restaurants or regions.

## Gift cards

Buy or redeem gift cards under **Account → Gift cards**. Redeemed gift cards become account credit
and are used automatically on future orders. Gift cards are non-refundable but don't expire.

## Receipts and invoices

Every completed order has an itemized receipt under **Orders → [order] → Receipt**, which you can
view or email to yourself. Receipts show items, fees, taxes, tip, discounts, and the payment method.

## FoodAssist Plus billing

FoodAssist Plus is **$9.99/month** and renews on your signup date. The **first month is free** for
new members. Benefits: **free delivery on orders over $15**, lower service fees, and member-only
offers. Manage or cancel under **Account → FoodAssist Plus → Manage membership**. Cancelling stops
future renewals; you keep benefits until the end of the current billing period. The monthly fee is
**non-refundable once a billing period starts**.
