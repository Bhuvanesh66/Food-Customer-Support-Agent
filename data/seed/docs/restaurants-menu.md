# Restaurants, Menus & Dietary Info

## Finding restaurants

Browse by cuisine, search for a dish or restaurant name, or use filters for **price**, **rating**, **delivery time**, **offers**, and **dietary** options (vegetarian, vegan, halal, gluten-free). Restaurants that deliver to your saved address are shown first.

## Why can't I see a restaurant?

A restaurant may not appear if it's **closed**, **outside your delivery radius**, or **temporarily paused** (e.g. too busy). Try a different delivery time, confirm your address, or check back later.

## Dietary and allergen information

Many menu items show dietary tags and allergen notes, but these are provided by the restaurant and may not be complete. **If you have a serious allergy, add a note at checkout and message the restaurant** — FoodAssist AI can't guarantee a dish is free of allergens.

## Customizing your order

Most items support customizations (size, sides, spice level, "no onions", etc.) on the item screen before adding to cart. Add anything the restaurant should know in **Order notes** at checkout.

## Group orders

Start a **Group order** from a restaurant page and share the link so others can add their items to the same cart. The organizer checks out and pays, or you can split the bill if the restaurant supports it.

## Minimum order and fees

Some restaurants have a minimum order value shown on their page. Delivery and service fees vary by restaurant and distance and are shown before you pay. FoodAssist Plus members get free delivery on eligible orders over $15.

## Rating your order

After delivery, rate the food and your courier separately under **Orders → Rate**. Low ratings with a reason help us and the restaurant improve, and may trigger a support follow-up.
