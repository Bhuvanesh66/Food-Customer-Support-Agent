# Restaurants, Menus & Dietary Info

## Finding restaurants

Browse by **cuisine**, search for a dish or restaurant name, or use filters for **price**,
**rating**, **delivery time**, **offers/discounts**, **distance**, and **dietary** options
(vegetarian, vegan, halal, kosher, gluten-free). Restaurants that deliver to your saved address are
shown first, sorted by relevance, rating, and ETA.

## Why can't I see a restaurant?

A restaurant may not appear because it's:
- **Closed** for the day or outside its operating hours,
- **Outside your delivery radius** for your saved address,
- **Temporarily paused** (too busy to accept new orders), or
- **Not yet a partner** in your area.
Try a different delivery time, switch to a closer saved address, or check back later. You can often
**schedule** an order for when a closed restaurant reopens.

## Delivery radius and distance

Each restaurant has a delivery radius based on distance and courier availability. Restaurants
farther away may not show, or may have a higher delivery fee. Changing your delivery address changes
which restaurants are available.

## Dietary and allergen information

Many menu items show **dietary tags** (veg, vegan, halal, gluten-free) and allergen notes, but these
are **provided by the restaurant and may not be complete or guaranteed**. **If you have a serious
allergy:** add a clear note at checkout, message the restaurant, and consider calling them directly.
FoodAssist AI cannot guarantee a dish is free of any allergen due to shared kitchens and prep areas.
Severe-allergy questions are escalated to a human agent.

## Customizing your order

Most items support customizations — size, sides, spice level, add-ons, "no onions", substitutions —
on the item screen before adding to cart. Use **Order notes** at checkout for anything the
restaurant should know (extra napkins, utensils, cooking preferences). Restaurants honor notes when
possible but can't always accommodate every request.

## Minimum order and fees

Some restaurants set a **minimum order value**, shown on their page and enforced at checkout.
Delivery and service fees vary by restaurant, distance, and demand and are always shown **before you
pay**. **FoodAssist Plus** members get **free delivery on eligible orders over $15**. Small orders
may carry a higher delivery fee.

## Out-of-stock items

If a restaurant marks an item out of stock after you order, they may contact you for a substitution
or the item is refunded. You can pre-approve substitutions in **Order notes** ("if sold out,
substitute with…").

## Groceries and convenience

Many areas have **grocery and convenience stores** on FoodAssist AI alongside restaurants. Look for
the **Grocery** category on the home screen. Grocery orders support substitutions and per-item
refunds for anything unavailable.

## Offers and discounts

Restaurant-specific offers (e.g. "20% off", "free item over $25") appear on the restaurant page and
apply automatically at checkout when conditions are met. Platform promo codes are entered separately
under **Add promo code**.

## Favorites and reordering

Tap the heart icon to **favorite** a restaurant. Reorder a past order in one tap from **Orders →
[past order] → Reorder**, which rebuilds the same cart.

## Rating and reviewing

After delivery, rate the **food** (and separately the **courier**). Reviews help other customers and
the restaurant. Consistently low-rated restaurants are reviewed by our partnerships team.
