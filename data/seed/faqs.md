# FoodAssist AI — Frequently Asked Questions

## Orders & Tracking

### How do I track my order?
Open the **Orders** tab and tap your active order. You'll see live status (Confirmed → Preparing →
Picked up → On the way → Delivered) and your courier on the map with an estimated arrival time.

### Where is my order?
Tap the order under **Orders** to see the live map and ETA. After pickup you can message the courier.
If it's more than 15 minutes past the estimate with no movement, contact support for help or a credit.

### How accurate is the delivery ETA?
It's an estimate based on prep time, distance, traffic, and weather, and updates as conditions
change. Peak times (weekend evenings, holidays) run longer.

### Can I order for someone else?
Yes. At checkout set a different delivery address and add the recipient's details and notes in
**Order notes**. You can also send **gift cards** under Account → Gift cards.

### Can I schedule an order in advance?
Yes — choose **Schedule** at checkout and pick a delivery window. Edit or cancel any time before the
restaurant starts preparing it.

### Can I add items after placing the order?
No — once placed, an order can't be edited. Cancel it (if still Confirmed) and reorder, or place a
second order.

### What happens if the restaurant is closed when I order?
You can't place an ASAP order at a closed restaurant, but you can **schedule** one for when it
reopens. If a restaurant closes after you order, we notify you and refund automatically.

## Cancellations & Changes

### How do I cancel my order?
Go to **Orders → Cancel order**. It's **free before the restaurant starts preparing**. After cooking
begins, cancellation isn't free — contact support; partial credit may apply.

### Will I be charged if I cancel?
Not if you cancel while the order is still **Confirmed**. After preparation starts, the food is being
made, so a charge or partial credit may apply.

### Can I change my delivery address after ordering?
Only before the courier picks up, and only to a nearby location, via **Orders → Edit delivery
details**. Large changes aren't possible once a courier is assigned.

### Can I change the restaurant after ordering?
No. Cancel (if still eligible) and place a new order from the other restaurant.

## Missing, Wrong & Quality Issues

### How do I report a missing item?
**Orders → [order] → Get help → Report an issue**, select the missing items, choose a resolution.
Approved refunds go to credit (instant) or your card (3–5 business days).

### My food arrived cold or damaged — what can I do?
Report it under **Get help → Report an issue → Food quality** (photos help). We issue a partial or
full refund depending on severity and may add a goodwill credit.

### I received the wrong order entirely.
Report it under **Get help**. We refund in full and help you re-order.

### The app says delivered but I didn't get it.
Check around your door and with neighbors, confirm your address, then report under **Get help →
Order never arrived**. We review the courier's GPS drop-off and make it right.

## Payments & Refunds

### Why was my payment declined?
Usually an expired card, insufficient funds, a bank fraud check, or wrong billing details. Update
your card under **Account → Payment** and retry, or use another method.

### What payment methods do you accept?
Credit/debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, gift cards, account credit, and UPI/wallets in supported
regions.

### Why is there a pending charge that disappeared?
That's a temporary **authorization hold** (your bank verifying the card). It isn't a real charge and
clears within a few days.

### I was charged twice.
One charge is likely the pending hold that drops off automatically. If both settle, report it under
**Get help → Billing** and we'll refund the duplicate.

### How do refunds work?
You choose **FoodAssist AI credit** (instant, never expires) or **your original payment method**
(3–5 business days). Eligibility is reviewed per order.

### How long does a refund take?
Credit is instant; card refunds take 3–5 business days (sometimes longer depending on your bank).

### My refund hasn't arrived.
Check the same card you paid with for a "FoodAssist" credit, confirm the refund was approved on the
order, and allow 3–5 business days. Still missing? Contact support with your order ID.

### Why is there a service fee / delivery fee?
The service fee helps operate the platform; the delivery fee covers courier delivery and varies by
distance and demand. FoodAssist Plus waives delivery on orders over $15.

### How do I get a receipt?
Every completed order has an itemized receipt under **Orders → [order] → Receipt** that you can view
or email.

## Promos, Credits & Gift Cards

### How do I use a promo code?
At checkout tap **Add promo code** and enter it. Most have a minimum order value and expiry and
can't be combined unless stated.

### My promo code isn't working.
Check the minimum order value, expiry date, whether it's first-order-only, and whether it's
restricted to certain restaurants or regions.

### Do credits expire?
No — FoodAssist AI account credit never expires and is applied automatically to your next order.

### How do gift cards work?
Buy or redeem under **Account → Gift cards**. Redeemed gift cards become account credit. Gift cards
don't expire but aren't refundable.

## Tipping

### How do I add or change a tip?
Add a tip at checkout, or adjust it from the order screen **up to 1 hour after delivery**. 100% of
tips go to your courier.

### Does the courier get my whole tip?
Yes — couriers receive 100% of tips.

## Account & Security

### How do I reset my password?
On the sign-in screen tap **Forgot password?**, enter your email, and open the reset link (valid 1
hour). New password needs at least 8 characters and one number.

### I didn't get the password reset email.
Check spam, confirm you used the right account email, and if you signed up by phone use **Sign in
with code** instead.

### How do I change my phone number or email?
Under **Account → Profile** — you'll verify the new contact with a code. Your history and credits
stay with the account.

### How do I delete my account?
**Account → Privacy → Delete account**. It's permanent after a 30-day grace period and forfeits any
credit, so use your balance first.

### I see an order I didn't place.
Change your password, sign out unknown devices under **Security → Devices**, and contact support to
freeze the account and refund unauthorized orders.

### How do I turn off promotional notifications?
**Account → Notifications** — mute promotions while keeping order and delivery alerts on.

## Restaurants & Menu

### Why can't I see a restaurant?
It may be closed, outside your delivery radius, temporarily paused (too busy), or not a partner in
your area. Try a different time or a closer address.

### Do you deliver groceries?
Yes — look for the **Grocery** category. Grocery orders support substitutions and per-item refunds.

### Is there a minimum order?
Some restaurants set a minimum order value, shown on their page. Small orders may have a higher
delivery fee.

### I have a food allergy — is it safe to order?
Allergen tags come from the restaurant and aren't guaranteed (shared kitchens). Add a note, message
the restaurant, and for serious allergies contact us — we'll connect you with a human agent.

### Can I customize a dish?
Yes, most items have options (size, sides, spice, substitutions) on the item screen, plus free-text
**Order notes** at checkout.

### How do I reorder a past order?
**Orders → [past order] → Reorder** rebuilds the same cart in one tap.

## Membership

### How much is FoodAssist Plus and what do I get?
$9.99/month for free delivery on orders over $15, lower service fees, and member offers. First month
free for new members.

### How do I cancel FoodAssist Plus?
**Account → FoodAssist Plus → Manage membership → Cancel.** Benefits continue until the end of the
billing period; the monthly fee is non-refundable mid-cycle.

## Couriers / Becoming a Driver

### How do I become a courier?
Sign up via the courier section of the app/site, provide the required details and documents, and pass
a quick background check. You need a smartphone and a mode of transport.

### How do couriers get paid?
Couriers earn per delivery plus 100% of tips, with payouts on a regular schedule. Specific rates vary
by region.

## Still need help?

If your question isn't covered here, chat with **Synapse AI** in the app or open **Orders → Get
help** on a specific order. We'll resolve it or connect you with a human support agent.
