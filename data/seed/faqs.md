# FoodAssist AI — Frequently Asked Questions

## How do I track my order?
Open the **Orders** tab and tap your active order. You'll see live status (Confirmed → Preparing → Picked up → On the way → Delivered) and your courier on the map with an estimated arrival time.

## My food arrived cold or damaged. What can I do?
Report it under **Orders → [your order] → Get help → Report an issue** and select the problem. Approved issues are refunded as FoodAssist AI credit (instant) or to your card (3–5 business days). Photos help us resolve it faster.

## Can I schedule an order for later?
Yes. At checkout, choose **Schedule** instead of ASAP and pick a delivery window. You can edit or cancel a scheduled order before the restaurant starts preparing it.

## How do I add or change a tip?
Add a tip at checkout, or adjust it from the order screen up to 1 hour after delivery. 100% of tips go to your courier.

## Why is there a small charge that disappeared?
That's a temporary **authorization hold** your bank places to verify the card. It isn't a real charge and clears within a few days; the final amount settles when your order completes.

## Do you deliver groceries?
Yes — many areas have grocery and convenience stores on FoodAssist AI alongside restaurants. Look for the **Grocery** category on the home screen.

## How do I use a promo code?
At checkout, tap **Add promo code** and enter it. Most codes have a minimum order value and an expiry date, and can't be combined unless stated.

## Can I order for someone else?
Yes. At checkout, set a different delivery address and add the recipient's details and any notes in **Order notes**. You can also send FoodAssist AI gift cards under **Account → Gift cards**.

## What if the restaurant is closed when I order?
You can't place an ASAP order at a closed restaurant, but you can **schedule** one for when it reopens. If a restaurant closes after you order, we'll notify you and refund automatically.

## Is there a minimum order?
Some restaurants set a minimum order value, shown on their page. There's no app-wide minimum, but small orders may have a higher delivery fee.
